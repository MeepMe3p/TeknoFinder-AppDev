Application Overview
WHAT'S TEKNOFINDER?
TeknoFinder is CIT-U's innovative campus map app designed for students, staff,
and guests. It offers real-time navigation, building directories, and detailed
guides to help users easily locate classrooms, offices, and key facilities.
Whether you're a newcomer or a regular on campus, TeknoFinder ensures
a smooth and hassle-free experience exploring CIT-U.

Faculty

Faculty members can use TeknoFinder to quickly locate classrooms, offices, and campus facilities, ensuring they stay on schedule and effortlessly navigate the CIT-U grounds.

◍ ☉☉

Students

TeknoFinder helps students efficiently find lecture halls, labs, and event venues, making it easier to attend classes, join activities, and explore the CIT-U campus stress-free.

◍ ◍ ☉

Guests

Guests and visitors can rely on TeknoFinder for seamless navigation, complete with building guides and real-time directions to key facilities and events across the CIT-U campus.
